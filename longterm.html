What Are The Longterm Effects Of Vaping

The long-term effects of vaping can encompass a range of health concerns. Respiratory problems such as chronic bronchitis and diminished lung function, as well as cardiovascular issues, are potential outcomes. Additionally, there's a risk of developing nicotine addiction, and emerging research suggests possible links to heightened risks of respiratory and heart diseases, although the comprehensive scope of these effects is still undergoing investigation
